Each provides all the infrastructure you need to deploy and serve your AI and ML models performantly, scalably, and cost-efficiently.
With Each you can: 

- Deploy and test your AI model and pipeline with **Magicpoint**, our open source CLI tool for developers.
- Use more than 120+ public models on **Each Model Garden** with just 1 click!
- Use our **Magicpoint SDK** to create unique applications with a few commands.
- Use our **Client SDKs** to communicate with your AI models in any language!
- Handle tons of traffic with **to the moon scaling** functionality.
- Save your infra cost with **scale to zero** and **lighting fast cold starts**.
- Manage your models' **deployment**, **health status**, **metrics**, **logs** and **spend** in your Each workspace.

## Getting Started
Every organization dashboard comes with 120 models and 70 ready-to-use AI workflow templates, allowing users to leverage public models and use our platform!
