### Inference Pricings

You can run your AI models with just one line of code. You pay only for the inference you use.

Each AI model runs on different types of hardware. So each AI model has a different price. You can check the price of each AI model in the Model Yard.

### Billing Cycle

Each billing cycle is monthly. You will be billed at the beginning of each month for the previous month's usage.

### Payment Methods

You can pay with credit card or bank transfer. We accept all major credit cards.

### Refund Policy

You can cancel your subscription at any time. 
If you cancel your subscription, you will not be billed for the next month. We do not offer refunds for the current month.
